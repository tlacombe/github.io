About me

I am a Ph.D. student in the DataShape team under the co-supervision of Steve Oudot (DataShape, Inria Saclay) and Marco Cuturi (CREST, Ensae - Google brain, Paris). Grant from AMX.

Research Interest

I am working on Topological Data Analysis (TDA), a recent field in data sciences which provides descriptors encoding the underlying topology of data. My research focuses on developing statistical and learning tools for these descriptors by making use of the well-developed Optimal Transport (OT) theory.

News

I am currently looking for post-doc opportunities. I am interested in metric geometry, statistical and learning problems in metric measure spaces (typically equipped with OT-like metrics).
