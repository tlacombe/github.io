Welcome on Théo Lacombe's webpage

I am a PhD student under the co-supervision of Steve Oudot (Datashape, Inria Saclay) and Marco Cuturi (CREST, Ensae). Grant from AMX.

Research Interest

I am working on Topological Data Analysis (TDA), a recent field in Data sciences which provides descriptors encoding the underlying topology of a given dataset. My research focuses on developping statistical and learning tools for these descriptors by making use of the well-developped Optimal Transport theory.
