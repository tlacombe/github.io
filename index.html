I am currently a post-doctoral researcher in the DataShape team for 6 months. Before that, I completed a Ph.D. in the same team, done under the co-supervision of Steve Oudot (DataShape, Inria Saclay) and Marco Cuturi (CREST, Ensae - Google brain, Paris). Grant from AMX.

I am working on interactions between Topological data analysis (TDA) and Optimal transport (OT). I am interested in both theoretical and computational aspects of these two fields, targetting applications in statistics and maching learning.
